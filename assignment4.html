Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

One problem with my challenge three is that there is no feedback when you submit a form. I know my form isn't coded to store any data, but it would be nice to have a page that says something like "Your form has been submitted!". Also, the form is only in english and has no language selection. For the chess challenge, it would be difficult for a blind person to follow along because there are no text cues on moves each person makes for the text reader to read. Also, it is a problem that both sides pieces are black as this could cause major confusion on whose pieces are whose.
